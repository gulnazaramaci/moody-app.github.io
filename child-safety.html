Child Safety Standards

Moody App is committed to preventing child sexual abuse and exploitation (CSAE). We have a zero-tolerance policy for any content or behavior that exploits or harms children.

1. Safety Measures

  • Users can report inappropriate content or behavior.
  • Users can block other users.
  • We actively monitor content to ensure community safety.

2. Content Moderation

We use both automated systems and manual review processes to detect and remove harmful or abusive content. Any violation results in immediate action, including content removal and account suspension.

3. Zero Tolerance Policy

Any form of child sexual abuse or exploitation is strictly prohibited. Accounts involved in such activities will be permanently banned and reported to relevant authorities when necessary.

4. Reporting

If you encounter any suspicious or harmful activity, please contact us immediately:

📧 safety@moodyapp.com

5. Cooperation with Authorities

We cooperate fully with law enforcement agencies to investigate and prevent child exploitation and abuse.
